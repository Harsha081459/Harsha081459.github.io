Traffic Sentinel AI

A production-deployed two-wheeler traffic violation detection system. Built a 3-stage YOLOv11 cascade — full-frame motorcycle detection, crop-level helmet classification, and license plate localisation — trained on 17,670 curated images from COCO, VisDrone, and Roboflow on a remote RTX 4060 Ti GPU. All inference is accelerated via ONNX Runtime (1.8× CPU speedup), served through a FastAPI backend, and deployed publicly on Hugging Face Spaces.

mAP50: 0.936 plate · 0.829 helmet · 0.761 motorcycle · 1.8× ONNX speedup
